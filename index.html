How the signal becomes useful

Peer2Park is not trying to predict parking forever. It is trying to improve the next decision.

Why freshness matters

The hardest part of parking is knowing whether the information is already old.

A map can show where parking exists in theory. Peer2Park is focused on a narrower problem: whether a recent opening is still actionable by the time the next driver gets there.

That is why the page is built around motion, footage, and timing. The point is not to make parking look futuristic. The point is to make recent street evidence feel more useful than stale confidence.
